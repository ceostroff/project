The Rising Cost of College

Since the 1970s, the cost of college has been rising rapidly across the country, according to the College Board While Florida's tuition increases have been relatively low compared with the national average, recent budget cuts to Florida's higher education could result in increased tuition in the future, according to the Research Institute on Social and Economic Policy.

So how much will college cost you? How much is it likely to increase by the time you finish college? Take the quiz and find out.
